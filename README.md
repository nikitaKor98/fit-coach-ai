# FitCoach AI

AI-powered fitness analytics platform that transforms health and training data from wearable devices into actionable insights and personalized coaching recommendations.

## Overview

FitCoach AI is designed to help users better understand their body, training performance, recovery, and long-term progress.

Modern wearable devices collect a huge amount of information, but most users struggle to interpret the data and make informed decisions.

FitCoach AI bridges this gap by combining data visualization, performance analytics, and AI-powered coaching.

## Vision

The long-term goal is to build a digital fitness coach that can:

- Analyze health and training data
- Detect trends and performance changes
- Identify potential recovery issues
- Recommend training adjustments
- Adapt to user goals and feedback
- Provide coaching-like guidance 24/7
